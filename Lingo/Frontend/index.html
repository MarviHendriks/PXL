Welcome to our game: Lingo

Feel free to register, login and play.

Below you can find the game rules:

  • Game rules
    •
  • A game consists of 4 puzzles
  • A puzzle is finished as a player guesses the correct word or if there have been 6 guesses
  • If a player made 5 guesses, he will lose the turn and the other player can guess 1 last time

  • If a player guesses correct, the player will be awarded with 25 points and the player can grab a ball
  • If the ball is blue, it will be crossed out on your card and you can grab another ball
  • If the player grabbed 2 blue balls, the player will keep his turn and can start the next puzzle
  • If the player grabs a red ball, the player loses his turn

  • When the word does not exist, you will also lose your turn
  • If a player gets lingo on his card, he will be awarded with 100 extra points
  • Lingo happens if all ball horizontal or vertical or diagonal are crossed out

  • The winner is the player who has the most points after 4 puzzles
